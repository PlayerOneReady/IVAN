Welcome To IVAN

Welcome to IVAN (Identifying Violation Affecting Neighbors) Imperial Valley, home of the first community-based Environmental Monitoring System.

IVAN is a community-based environmental enforcement reporting site that connects resident of the Imperial Valley with the proper agencies that can help solve local environmental problems. Whether you're at home, work, or at play, IVAN is available to you and your community to document environmental concerns. To get started choose one of the options below:

Submit A Report

Report an environmental issue in a few easy steps

Local Reports

View, filter and explore hundreds of user submitted reports

Air Monitoring

A new IVAN tool which allows users to monitor local air levels and learn what they mean
